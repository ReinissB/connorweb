I want to tell you a story... Mine...

Music was always part of my life. I don't know the how's or why's but the music just bursts the way of living life: singing, playing, listening.

The new single "All-Giving" is out now!

I arrived in Aalborg, thrilled, but my fresh high hopes started to fade when the Sun didn't appear for nine long days. When it did I saw the sacred, God. The writing came fluently as never before. Meeting with Fran De Pian was great to record it mixing our styles.
