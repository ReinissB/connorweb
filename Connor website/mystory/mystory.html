I sing what the Universe brings. There is neither plan nor structure to express myself. Some songs tell experiences and reflections I had, others are fictions that happened or eventually will do so.

A baby is born

I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, April 1st 1992. My mother says the moment she saw me I was staring at her and all around with my big eyes. From the moment they saw me they said that I was a very curious person.

Let there be rock

CD’s and cassettes brought magic within: The Beatles, Guns N’ Roses, Queen, Nirvana, Red Hot Chili Peppers. Inevitably, I got inspired. I got my first guitar for my 12th birthday. It was either that or a school trip. I reckon I decided well. Now it was time to start to unblock Rock riffs and songs.

First band, first gigs

I was lucky to form great bands with friends from school and my music institute. Nirmods, Awoodstock, Full Throttle, the Warhols. Funny and happy times. But also courageous, playing at Parque de la Costa, Doors, Marin, Canal 7, and more.

For many years I didn’t have time nor money to record. But 2020 brought the opposite. All the songs I had been creating exploded like a waterfall…

Historias de esta tierra

My first album is loaded with emotion. I can’t express entirely the joy of producing with friends. Stories, fictions and personal experiences mark this album. Powerful emotions are present, they had to be sang out loud.

Before north

/ Antes del norte

I couldn’t cut the flow of music. And I didn’t want it to. So just before going to Denmark I rushed to record a double album. It was also stirring: I was leaving home to embark on a new adventure. “Before North” is energetic and mystical. You might be carried away easily by the thread of the album.

Before north /

Antes del norte

Painted with an acoustic sunny vibe it also passes through an introspective momentum.
